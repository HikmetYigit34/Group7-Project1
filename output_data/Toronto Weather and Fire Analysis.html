Toronto Weather and Fire Analysis

Objective

  • Review Toronto maximum temperatures in the last 10 years, check if increasing steadily,
  • Review Toronto fire incident counts within the same period of time , check if increasing steadily,
  • Try to correlate if there is a significant relation between max temp increase and fire incident increase.

Data Source for Weather

    Data is from Government of Canada website, Environment and Natural Resources, Weather, Climate and Hazard section.
    Starting from 2013 to 2022, 120(10years*12months) csv files for each month with hourly granulation.
    Total of 120 csv file. Each file is about 750rows and 30 columns
    Overall data is 90000rows and 30 columns.

Summary of Max Temperatures of the Months
